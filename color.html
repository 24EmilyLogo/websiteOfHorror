Color Theory in Horror:
How color changes the mood of different scenes

Why does color matter?

As with any art, video, and or design, color matters. Having colors can help get the feeling the makers want the audience to feel accross without having to use words. Color also changes the overall tone of the scene to make the scene more coherent. There are many ways to handle coloring and many ways to experiment with colors.
