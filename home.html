What is Horror?

Horror is a genre set on being unsettling, scary, and suspenseful. Horror is use in many different mediums including but not limited to: video games, movies, books, & plays. "The genre goes back to Ancient Greece and Rome, where stories in the horror genre were related to death, demons, evil and vengeful spirits and the afterlife. There are 6 sub-genres of horror: gothic, paranormal, occult, dark fantasy, survival, and science fiction horror," (Masterclass).
